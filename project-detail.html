Third-year interactive design course final project, focused on creating user experience (UX) and app UI design related to events. I chose "Graduation Music Festival" as the event theme and designed an app and event to enhance users' experience of participating in the event. Through this project, I learned how to start from user needs, conduct UX research, information architecture design, and UI visual design, ultimately creating a digital platform that promotes social interaction and event participation.

大三上互動設計期末專案，主題為創造活動相關使用者體驗UX與App UI設計。我以「畢業音樂祭」為活動主題，設計了一款App與活動，旨在提升使用者參與活動的體驗。透過這個專案，我學習了如何從使用者需求出發，進行UX研究、資訊架構設計以及UI視覺設計，最終創造出一個能夠促進社交互動和活動參與的數位平台。
