關於我 About Me

我是劉愛，一位專注於UI/UX和Web Design的設計師，致力於創造引人入勝且功能完善的數位體驗。我的設計哲學根植於對視覺傳達的熱愛，並且我相信每個設計決策都應該服務於更大的敘事和用戶需求。 With a background in Visual Communication, I view design as a process of excavation: uncovering clarity and potential layer by layer. I specialize in bridging the gap between aesthetics and digital functionality. My focus lies in UI/UX and Web Design, where I leverage my visual expertise to transform complex ideas into intuitive, interactive, and meaningful digital experiences.
