關於我 About Me

我是劉愛。專注於視覺傳達設計，並積極實踐於 UI/UX 和 Web Design領域。我認為設計不只是外在的修飾，更是對使用者需求的精確回應。我喜歡挑戰如何在複雜的邏輯中，梳理出簡單且優雅的視覺秩序。 I am Liu Aiesha. My focus is on Visual Communication Design, with an active practice in the fields of UI/UX and Web Design. I believe that design is not merely external embellishment, but a precise response to user needs. I enjoy the challenge of distilling a simple and elegant visual order from complex logic.
